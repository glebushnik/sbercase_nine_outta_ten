## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Product Overview
The AI Copilot platform enables business users to generate, validate, and execute workflows based on natural language input, with Langflow serving as the execution engine. The platform simplifies complex business tasks, eliminating the need for developer involvement. 

As a **Domain Expert (CX / Support / Ops)**, your role is to ensure that the platform meets the needs of **customers**, **support teams**, and **operations**. You will be responsible for working with users and stakeholders to define business requirements, identify user pain points, and provide guidance on how to maximize the platform’s potential. Additionally, you will play a key role in gathering feedback, improving the platform, and ensuring its success in various business environments.

---

## 2. Key Responsibilities of the Domain Expert

### 2.1 Define Business Requirements and User Needs
You will be the bridge between the **business** and the development teams, ensuring that the platform meets real-world business needs. This involves:
- **Gathering requirements** from different business teams and **translating them** into actionable product features.
- Working with the **Product Manager** to prioritize features that deliver the most value to users.
- Ensuring that business teams are aligned on the platform's goals and its intended impact.

#### Deliverables:
- **Business requirements document** outlining key user needs and platform objectives.
- **Feature prioritization** based on business value.
- **User personas** for understanding different business stakeholders (e.g., CX leads, support teams).

### 2.2 Gather Feedback and Improve User Experience
You will work closely with the **UX/Product Designer** to gather feedback and continuously improve the user experience. This includes:
- Conducting **user interviews** and **surveys** to collect feedback on the platform’s usability.
- Identifying pain points and areas of friction within the platform and providing suggestions for improvement.
- Assisting in the **user journey mapping** process to ensure that the platform aligns with user expectations.

#### Deliverables:
- **User feedback reports** summarizing key findings from user interviews, surveys, and usability tests.
- **Pain point identification** and recommendations for UX improvements.
- **User journey maps** outlining the steps users take to complete their tasks on the platform.

### 2.3 Provide Guidance to Support Teams and Operations
As a domain expert, you will work closely with the **CX (Customer Experience)**, **support**, and **operations** teams to ensure that the platform’s implementation and support processes are smooth. You will:
- **Train** support teams to troubleshoot and resolve issues effectively.
- Provide **guidance** on how the platform can be best utilized in different business operations.
- Work with **operations teams** to ensure the platform is scalable and supports business workflows.

#### Deliverables:
- **Support documentation** to help support teams troubleshoot common issues.
- **Training materials** for CX and support teams on platform usage and best practices.
- **Best practice guidelines** for optimizing platform usage within business operations.

### 2.4 Monitor Platform Adoption and Success
You will monitor how well the platform is being adopted by various business teams and ensure that it is delivering on its promise. This includes:
- Tracking **usage metrics** to measure the platform's effectiveness in solving business problems.
- Collecting **user success stories** to demonstrate the platform's impact.
- Working with the **Product Manager** and **Data Team** to analyze platform usage and identify opportunities for improvement.

#### Deliverables:
- **Usage reports** tracking platform adoption and engagement metrics.
- **User success stories** to highlight the value delivered by the platform.
- **Adoption improvement plan** based on feedback and usage data.

### 2.5 Advocate for Platform Improvements and Feature Requests
You will be the **advocate** for business teams and users, ensuring that their needs are reflected in the product roadmap. This involves:
- **Collecting feature requests** and feedback from various stakeholders.
- Working with the **Product Manager** to prioritize enhancements and new features.
- Communicating the business value of new features and advocating for their inclusion in the platform.

#### Deliverables:
- **Feature request backlog** with business impact analysis.
- **Prioritized feature list** for platform improvements.
- **Business case documentation** for new features based on user feedback.

### 2.6 Ensure Successful Platform Launch and Rollout
You will play a key role in ensuring the **successful launch** of the platform, including:
- Coordinating with **operations teams** to ensure the platform is ready for business-wide use.
- Ensuring that **training** and **onboarding** materials are in place for new users.
- Helping to **manage platform rollout** to ensure smooth adoption and minimal disruption.

#### Deliverables:
- **Launch plan** detailing rollout and user adoption strategies.
- **Onboarding materials** for business teams to get up to speed quickly.
- **Go-live support** to ensure a smooth transition from development to production.

### 2.7 Continuous Platform Improvement
Post-launch, you will continue to monitor how the platform is performing and drive continuous improvement. This involves:
- **Collecting user feedback** post-launch to identify areas for improvement.
- Working with the **Product Manager** and **UX team** to iterate on the product and enhance features.
- Driving **platform updates** based on business needs and evolving requirements.

#### Deliverables:
- **Post-launch feedback reports** to track issues and opportunities for improvement.
- **Platform update recommendations** based on user feedback and business requirements.
- **Continuous improvement roadmap** to prioritize enhancements and updates.

## 4. Success Metrics for Domain Expert

### 4.1 Business Metrics
- **Platform adoption rate**: The percentage of users actively using the platform.
- **User satisfaction**: Based on surveys and feedback from business teams.
- **Return on investment (ROI)**: Business impact achieved through workflow automation and task execution.

### 4.2 User Experience Metrics
- **Task completion rate**: The percentage of tasks successfully completed by users.
- **Time to completion**: How long it takes users to complete their tasks on the platform.
- **Error rate**: How often users encounter issues or bugs in the platform.

### 4.3 Feature Success Metrics
- **Feature usage**: How often new features are used by business teams.
- **Feature adoption rate**: Percentage of business teams using newly implemented features.
- **Feature feedback score**: How well new features align with business needs.
